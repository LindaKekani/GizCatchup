My Journey Into IT

My interest in It began in high school when I did CAT. One time they told us about how one can create a basic web page using html, but unfortunately, we didn't get into it.

I went to a university in Pretoria and did Biochemistry. It was a lot but I enjoyed it.

A few years later, I took a STATA course. After that, I learnt a little bit about data science, during class I kept hearing about Python, so I decided to do some research on it.
This sparked that interest I had back in High School

My nephews and I play a lot of games together since I spend a lot of time with my family, and their curiosity and our discussions about games- such as how we could change somethings from there- has fueled my interest into game development

The thought of being a game developer excites and motivates me to continue pushing boundaries and reaching for new heights.

My Goals

Who said you cant be great in different fields??

My goals are simple. Be the best version of myself, and be one of the greatests in academia and as a developer,
Create games suitable for kids of all ages, games I would let my future kids play.

The process of self-discovery never ends, and every stage of my life has shaped the person I am now. My identity is shaped by my never-ending curiosity,
enjoyment of many artistic mediums, endurance in the face of adversity, and awareness of the interconnectedness of all things. I am eager to continue weaving the beautiful tapestry of my life with the threads of experiences that will still be experienced as I stand on the edge of the future.
